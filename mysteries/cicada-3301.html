Cicada 3301: The Internet's Hardest Puzzle

Cicada 3301 is the name given to an enigmatic organization that, on three occasions, posted a set of complex puzzles and alternate reality games to recruit 'highly intelligent individuals' from the public.

History and Famous Sightings

The first puzzle appeared in 2012. It required knowledge of steganography, cryptography, and ancient philosophy. Those who followed the trail were led to physical locations across the globe and deep into the 'Dark Web.'

Scientific and Popular Theories

Is it a recruitment tool for the CIA or NSA? A secret society of hackers? Or just a highly sophisticated art project? The true identity and purpose of Cicada 3301 remain unknown.

Cultural Impact and Legacy

Cicada 3301 set the gold standard for internet mysteries, proving that the digital world still holds secrets that can challenge even the brightest minds.
