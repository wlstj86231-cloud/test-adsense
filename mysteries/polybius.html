Polybius: The Game That Never Was?

Polybius is a legendary 1980s arcade game rumored to have been a government-run psychology experiment that caused amnesia, insomnia, and night terrors in its players.

ADVERTISEMENT

History and Famous Sightings

According to the legend, the game appeared in a few arcades in Portland, Oregon, in 1981. It was said to be extremely addictive, but players soon suffered from horrific side effects. Men in black suits were allegedly seen collecting data from the machines before they vanished entirely.

Scientific and Popular Theories

Most historians believe Polybius is an elaborate urban legend. It might have been inspired by real-world events, such as the FBI investigating arcades for gambling or actual arcade games that caused seizures (like Tempest).

Cultural Impact and Legacy

Polybius has become the ultimate 'creepypasta' for the gaming world, appearing in 'The Simpsons' and various modern indie games that pay homage to its mysterious aura.
