The Roswell Incident: The Birth of a Legend

In July 1947, something crashed in the desert near Roswell, New Mexico. The military's initial report of a 'flying disc' was quickly retracted, sparking the world's most famous UFO conspiracy theory.

ADVERTISEMENT

History and Famous Sightings

For decades, the event was largely forgotten until researchers in the 1970s began interviewing witnesses who claimed to have seen alien bodies and indestructible debris. The government later claimed it was a top-secret Project Mogul spy balloon.

Scientific and Popular Theories

Was it a genuine extraterrestrial spacecraft? A secret military experiment? Or a massive cover-up of a nuclear detection system? The debate continues to polarize the public and fuel distrust in government.

Cultural Impact and Legacy

Roswell is the 'Mecca' of UFOlogy, turning a small desert town into a global destination and cementing the 'Grey Alien' in the public imagination.
