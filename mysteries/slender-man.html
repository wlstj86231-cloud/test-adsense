Slender Man: The Tall Man of the Internet

Slender Man is a tall, thin, featureless figure in a black suit who is said to stalk and abduct children. Unlike most legends, his origin is perfectly documented: he was created on an internet forum in 2009.

ADVERTISEMENT

History and Famous Sightings

Created by Eric Knudsen (Victor Surge) for a Photoshop contest, Slender Man quickly became a viral sensation, evolving through thousands of stories, games, and fan-made videos into a genuine modern myth.

Scientific and Popular Theories

Slender Man is a fascinating example of 'open-source folklore.' He represents the modern equivalent of the 'Bogeyman,' adapted for the digital age where information—and fear—spreads at the speed of light.

Cultural Impact and Legacy

Slender Man's legacy is complicated by real-world tragedies inspired by the myth, but he remains a landmark in the study of how new legends are born in the 21st century.
